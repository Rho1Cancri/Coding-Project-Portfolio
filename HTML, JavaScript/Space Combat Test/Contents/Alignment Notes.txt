=== Lawful Good: Federation ===
Humans and other species who are united for collective defence of liberty.

=== Chaotic Good: Milquans ===
Tall, semi-reptilian humanoids who prefer bliss over serious business.

=== True Neutral: Asteroids ===
Floating rocks that don't do much but pose as hazards for unwary spaceships.

=== Lawful Evil: Empire ===
Humans who prefer to exert absolute power, but in an orderly and rational manner.

=== Chaotic Evil: Parasites ===
They are a semi-intelligent organism that infects any other species to propagate itself.
